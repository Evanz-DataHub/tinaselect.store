Create a free Firebase project
💡 Firebase is free for small businesses. The free Spark plan gives you 1GB storage, 50,000 reads/day, and 20,000 writes/day — more than enough for TinaSelect.
Steps to follow
1
Open console.firebase.google.com in a new tab and sign in with your Google account.
2
Click "Add project" or "Create a project".
3
Enter a project name e.g. tinaselect-store. Disable Google Analytics (not needed). Click Create project.
4
Wait for the project to be created, then click Continue. You'll land on the project dashboard.
🔥 Open Firebase Console ↗
2
Register your web app & get config
Steps to follow
1
On your Firebase project dashboard, click the web icon </> to add a web app.
2
Enter an app nickname e.g. TinaSelect. Leave "Firebase Hosting" unchecked. Click Register app.
3
You'll see a firebaseConfig code block. Copy everything inside the curly braces { }.
4
The config looks like this — you'll paste it in Step 4:
const firebaseConfig = {
  apiKey: "AIzaSyXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX",
  authDomain: "tinaselect.firebaseapp.com",
  projectId: "tinaselect-store",
  storageBucket: "tinaselect-store.appspot.com",
  messagingSenderId: "123456789012",
  appId: "1:123456789012:web:abc123def456"
};
3
Enable Firestore & Storage
🗄️
Firestore Database
Saves your products & prices
1. In the Firebase console left menu, click Firestore Database
2. Click Create database
3. Choose Start in test mode
4. Select a location (any), click Enable
📦
Firebase Storage
Stores your product photos
1. In the left menu, click Storage
2. Click Get started
3. Choose Start in test mode
4. Select a location, click Done
⚠️ Important: "Test mode" allows open access for 30 days — enough to get started. You can secure it later in Firebase Rules when you're ready.

Our Story

From a Passion for Sewing to Ghana's Favourite Handmade Brand

TinaSelect started the way most great things do — with passion, a needle and thread, and a dream. What began as making clothes for friends and family quickly grew into something much bigger as word spread about the quality, the fit, and the love put into every piece.

Today, TinaSelect serves hundreds of women across Ghana, crafting everything from elegant wrap dresses and bold Ankara two-pieces to everyday tops and chic palazzo trousers. Each item is made to order — meaning it's made specifically for you, in your size, in your chosen colour.

🌍
Our Mission

Celebrating African Women Through Fashion

We believe fashion should celebrate who you are — your curves, your style, your culture. That's why we offer sizes 6 through 20, and work with a wide range of fabrics from contemporary prints to vibrant Ankara and Kente-trimmed designs.

Our mission is simple: to make every Ghanaian woman feel beautiful, confident, and seen — at a price that makes sense. No compromise on quality, no compromise on you.
