Our Story

Born from a passion for fabric and a love for Ghanaian craftsmanship — every stitch tells a story.

How It Began

Handmade from the Heart

TinaSelect was born out of a deep love for fashion and a desire to create pieces that make women feel truly special. What started as a small project making clothes for friends and family has grown into a full collection of handmade garments that celebrate the beauty of Ghanaian women.


Every item is cut and sewn by hand — no mass production, no shortcuts. Just skill, love, and an eye for beautiful detail.

👗
Our Mission

Style That Fits You

We believe every woman deserves clothes that fit perfectly and feel amazing. That's why we offer custom sizing on every item — because fashion should work for your body, not the other way around.


From Ankara dresses to palazzo trousers, crop tops to two-piece sets — every style is handcrafted with premium fabric and meticulous attention to detail.

✂️
Our Values

Quality, Care & Community

We source quality fabrics and use techniques passed down through generations. Every garment is inspected before it leaves our hands, because your satisfaction is our greatest achievement.


When you buy TinaSelect, you're supporting a small Ghanaian business run with passion and purpose.
